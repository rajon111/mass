Mass Data Limited Annual Tour-2021

Mass Data Limited Annual Tour-2021 was celebrated on December24,2021. Mass Data Limited organizes such tours every year for its esteemed staff. All the officers and employees of Mass Data participated in the event spontaneously. The Annual Party was held in Cafe Rio Restaurant in Jassimuddin. Company arranged a buffet Dinner in this Restaurant.

Mass Data Limited Annual Tour's Pictures.

Honorable Advisor & Admin of Mass Data Limited Mr. Yakub Mohammad Abdullah joined Annual Office Tour-2021. Also present were Mr. Shakil Ahamed, CEO of Mass Data Limited Speaking to all the employees on the occasion of Annual Tour-2021, The CEO of Mass Data said that “Mass Data has always been working to give the employees their work as well as their emotional well-being. Because if the employees are mentally cheerful, they will be able to provide the highest quality service. With the hard work and dedication of every employee, Alpha Net has been successfully providing quality service to all its customers for over 04 years. He also urged to provide highest quality service to every customer in future also. Thanks to the love of Allah Ta’ala and the prayers of the customers, Mass Data has been able to organize such an event and we are hopeful that it will be able to organize such an event in the future as well.”
